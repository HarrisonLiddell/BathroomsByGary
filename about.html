About Me

Bathrooms By Gary is an experienced business, within bathroom renovations, kitchen renovations and lots of other wide range of skills. Bathrooms By Gary is based in the Northwest (including areas such as, Wigan, St Helens, Leigh, Warrington, Bolton, Liverpool & Manchester).

I am a time served joiner in all sorts of room renovations, but have always had a passion within the hands on industry. I strive to ensure that all my clients are satisfied and thus will attest to my superior craftmanship, attention to detail, clear communication, honesty and integrity.

I am always happy to help with the design of your kitchen or bathroom but i always like to make the process as enjoyable as possible for my customers. So please feel free to contact me to discuss your ideas.
